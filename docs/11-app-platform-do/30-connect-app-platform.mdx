# App Platform (DO) - Connecting on [DeployPRO](https://deploypro.dev)

You need to log in to your DeployPRO Account before connecting to any cloud provider. Log in to your account.

<br />

## Connection - Connect to DigitalOcean
You need to connect your [DeployPRO](https://deploypro.dev/) with your cloud provider. Do the connection in **`Connections Menu`**.

> You have the ability to disconnect a connected AWS account.

1. In the `Connection Menu`, click `Connect` in the AWS section.

  ![DeployPRO - Connect to DO]

<br />

2. Copy and paste your `DO Token` on the form provided.

  ![DeployPRO - App Platform Token Form]
  
<br />

3. Click `Connect` and wait until the process is finished.

  ![DeployPRO - Fill out AWS Form]

<br />

4. Upon success, the view will look like the picture below.

  ![DeployPRO - App Platform Service is Connected]

<br />
