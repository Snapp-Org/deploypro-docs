# Getting Started

In this technical documentation, we will guide you through the process of registering on Deploypro, enabling you to get started with deploying and managing your applications on this powerful platform.

## Step 1: Accessing the Deploypro Website
To begin the registration process, open your web browser and navigate to the Deploypro website at https://www.deploypro.dev/. Once there, click on the "Sign up" button located in the top right corner or in the middle of the page.

[picture]

## Step 2: Create a Deploypro Account with GitHub
On the sign-up page, right now you have only the option to register using your GitHub account, click on the "Log in with GitHub" button and follow the prompts to authorize the connection.

[picture]

## Step 3: Welcome to Deploypro
After allowing the authorization to your GitHub Account, you have completed the registration on Deploypro. Now, you can start deploying your project!

[picture]
